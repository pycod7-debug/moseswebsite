Since 1906

About Us

Founded in 1906, King's College Budo stands as a beacon of academic excellence and leadership in East Africa. For over a century, we have nurtured minds that have gone on to shape the political, social, and economic landscape of the region.

Our philosophy is rooted in holistic education. Through a balance of world-class academics, spiritual guidance in our historic Chapel, and competitive sports, we ensure every student emerges as a well-rounded citizen ready to lead.

"Gakyali Mabaga" — It is still the beginning. This motto reflects our commitment to continuous effort and the understanding that there is always more to achieve.

Fund Definitions

Student Fees & Financial Hardship

Financial hardship affects many students and can negatively impact performance. This platform allows alumni and well-wishers to provide direct support, such as the UGX 2,750,000 required for Senior Four students.

Sports Development

Successful sports programs require resources like equipment and coaching. Contributions empower our athletes to train effectively without the burden of raising capital themselves.

The Chapel Project

Infrastructure development is vital for spiritual growth. With a school of 2,800, we aim to bridge the funding gap for the fifteen billion shilling chapel project.
